About the Skatepark

The Burnham Park Skating Rink is a community-friendly open space dedicated to recreational and inline skating. Designed for skaters of all ages and skill levels, the rink offers a smooth concrete surface ideal for casual laps, practicing new moves, or just enjoying time on wheels with friends and family. Located in the fresh, elevated atmosphere of Baguio City, the skating rink is surrounded by greenery, creating a peaceful environment that makes every skating session both fun and refreshing. Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or an experienced skater working on your technique, the Burnham Park Skating Rink is a safe and welcoming place to roll.

Rink Features

  • Smooth Flat Concrete Surface
  • Spacious Layout
  • Beginner-Friendly Environment
  • Accessible Location
  • Ideal for Group Activities
  • Open platform on sides
  • Benches on the side

Park Rules

1. Skate at your own risk

2. Skating Rink hours 10am till 5-6pm

3. Keep the rink clean and dispose trash properly

4. Helmets, pads, safety gears are strongly recommended

5. Be aware and couteous of other riders

6. Keep all gums, foods, drinks and glass out of the use area

7. No fighting, vandalism, weapons or profanity

8. No drugs, tobacco products, e-cigarettes, and alcohol on or around the premises

9. Parents are encourage to be in attendance at all times if user is under the age of 10

10. Violation of these rules may result in suspension from use of the facility
