About the Federation



Mission Statement


We aim to nurture the talents of action sports athletes, create a vibrant sports community,
and provide opportunities for growth through events, competitions, and educational programs.



Why Partner with Us?

1. Showcase how sponsorship helps not just the athletes but also builds brand visibility for the sponsor.

2. Your sponsorship enables us to organize world-class events, maintain our skatepark, and foster the growth of skateboarding, BMX, and inline sports.



Benefits of Sponsorship

  • Logo Placement: Logos featured on event banners, promotional materials, website, and social media.
  • Event Promotion: Your brand promoted during live events, including mentions by event hosts and logo presence in marketing materials.
  • Online Presence: Sponsors will be prominently displayed on the BASF website and event listings.
